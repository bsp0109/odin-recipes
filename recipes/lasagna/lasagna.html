Lasagna Recipe

Description

A layered pasta dish made with ground beef, tomato sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella. The ground beef is browned in a skillet with garlic and herbs, then simmered with crushed tomatoes to create a rich and tangy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (1 lb)
  • Canned crushed tomatoes (28 oz)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (3 cloves)
  • Basil (1 tsp dried)
  • Oregano (1 tsp dried)
  • Ricotta cheese (15 oz)
  • Parmesan cheese (1 cup shredded)
  • Large eggs
  • Parsley (chopped, for garnish)
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded, for serving)
  • Lasagna noodles (12-16 sheets)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Tomato Sauce: Brown the ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat, breaking it up into small pieces as it cooks. Add garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper to taste, and cook for an additional minute.
  2. Simmer the Tomato Sauce: Stir in the canned crushed tomatoes, olive oil, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Bring the sauce to a simmer and let it cook for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has thickened and reduced slightly.
  3. Prepare the Ricotta Mixture: In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Spread a layer of tomato sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 4-6 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, followed by half of the ricotta mixture and half of the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  5. Repeat the Layers: Repeat steps 4, ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese on top. Cover the dish with aluminum foil.
  6. Bake the Lasagna: Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Serve and Enjoy!: Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with chopped parsley, if desired.
